Check the amount you pay for childcare

If the Tax Free Childcare (TFC) scheme helps with the costs of your childcare, you cannot take the amount they pay off your earnings.

For example, you pay £50 a week and TFC tops up £10. We can take the £50 you pay off your earnings when working out if you can get Carer's Allowance.
