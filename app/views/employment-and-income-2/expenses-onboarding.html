Your expenses from JOB NAME

Taking off expenses could reduce your weekly income so you earn less than the £196 a week limit to be eligible for Carer's Allowance.

We only allow you to claim expenses for things that you need to do your job.

Example of expenses

  • use your car while doing your job, you can claim a mileage allowance.
  • purchase a work uniform or clothing that has an employer's logo, or an item that is used exclusively for work (not worn for personal use).
  • work from home and pay for electricity (you can claim some of the cost as an expense).

Examples of things that you cannot treat as expenses

  • fuel for travelling to or from work.
  • cost of clothing that you can wear at work or home.
  • food costs.
  • full cost of household bills as some of this is for private use.
  • Internet use or WiFi
