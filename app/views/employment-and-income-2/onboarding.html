Your income

To be eligible for Carers Allowance, you must earn less than £196 a week.

To understand if you are eligible we will ask about your:

  • current income
  • income from any jobs that ended since DATE
  • all your jobs

If you are employed

We get your income and job details from DWP and HMRC records.

If your income is above £196 a week, you can reduce it by telling us about:

  • expenses you have to do your job
  • care costs for Joe Bloggs
  • childcare costs
  • contributions you make into a private or personal pension

If you are self-employed

You will need to send us your accounts or provide evidence about your weekly income.

If your income is above £196 a week, you can reduce it by telling us about:

  • care costs for Joe Bloggs
  • childcare costs
  • contributions you make into a private or personal pension

If you have other income

You can tell us about:

  • cash in hand
  • direct payments from Joe Bloggs (money they get from the local authority to buy their care)
  • Fostering Allowance
  • rental property
