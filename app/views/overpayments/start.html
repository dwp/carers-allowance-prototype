Request a review of your Carer's Allowance overpayment

We are reviewing Carer’s Allowance overpayments related to employed earnings. The Sayce Independent Review found inconsistencies in the way we worked out average earnings for carers.

You do not need to do anything. We will contact you if your case was affected.

Who can request a review

You can request a review if you think this affects you and you have not had a letter from DWP.

Who cannot request a review

Carers who were solely self-employed between 10 April 2015 and 2 September 2025.

When can you request a review

You can do this from 1 April 2026.

What you will need to request a review online

You will need your personal details and National Insurance number.
