Request a review of your Carer's Allowance overpayment

Only use this form if you have been asked to repay Carer's Allowance because of an overpayment related to employed earnings.

Who can request a review

This form is for carers who:

  • received a Carer's Allowance overpayment due to employed earnings that went over the limit between 10 April 2015 and 2 September 2025
  • are repaying DWP or have already paid back money because of an employed earnings overpayment

If you had more than one overpayment during this time, you only need to request a review once.

Who cannot request a review

Carers who were solely self-employed between 10 April 2015 and 2 September 2025.

When can you request a review

From 20 November 2025, you can request that we review your overpayment.

Request a review online

You will need your personal details and National Insurance number.
