Confirm your identity

You will need information from 2 sources to confirm your identity online.

You can choose from:

  • a valid UK passport
  • a UK driving licence (you cannot use a Northern Ireland driving licence)
  • a payslip from the last 3 months
  • a current P60
  • your Self Assessment tax return - the most recent payment you made
  • a current tax credits claim - a payment you received in the last 3 months
  • your credit record - for example, the date you opened a bank account

Do you have at least 2 of the above sources of information to confirm your identity online?

If not, we will contact you to confirm your identity before we process your claim.
