If you do not have access to a bank or building society account

You cannot apply for Carer's Allowance online without giving us details of an account we can pay into.

You can find advice about how to open an account on the MoneyHelper website (opens in a new tab).

If you are unable to open an account, you can apply for Carer's Allowance by post (opens in a new tab). We will need to contact you about your payment details before your claim can be processed.
