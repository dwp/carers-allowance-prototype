Scottish qualifying benefits 2024 and 2025

There are 2 new qualifying benefits being introduced in CA for people living in Scotland. The first is Pension Age Disability Payment (PADP) in October 2024. This is the equivalent of AA.

The second is Scottish Agency Disability Living Allowance (SADLA) in February 2025. This is the equivalent of DLA working age and cases classed as DLA 65+.

See Jira tickets ACA-3078 and ACA-3080.
