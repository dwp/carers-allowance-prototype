You cannot get Carer's Allowance if you do not provide care for at least 35 hours a week

You can only get Carer's Allowance if you spend at least 35 hours a week providing care for someone.

Other support available

If you spend at least 20 hours providing care for someone, you may be able to apply for Carer's Credit instead.

For help and advice about being a carer, you can also visit the Carers UK and Carers Trust websites.
