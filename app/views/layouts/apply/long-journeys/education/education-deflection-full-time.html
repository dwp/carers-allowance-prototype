You cannot get Carer's Allowance if you are in full-time education

As you doing a course of education which is classed as full-time, you are not able to get Carer's Allowance.

Other support available

You can ask your tutor, teacher or lecturer about what help may be available to you as a carer while you are in full-time education.

You can also get help and advice about being a carer from Carers UK and Carers Trust.
