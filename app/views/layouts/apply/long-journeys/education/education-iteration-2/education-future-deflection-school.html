You cannot get Carer's Allowance if you are still in school

As you will be at school and in full-time education from your claim date, you will not be able to get Carer's Allowance. You may be eligible for Carer's Allowance after you leave full-time education.

Other support available

You can get advice and support about being a young carer from The Children's Society (opens in a new tab) and the NHS (opens in a new tab).

You can also ask your teacher or tutor about what help may be available to you as a young carer.
