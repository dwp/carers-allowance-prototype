You cannot get Carer's Allowance if you are in full-time education

As you will be doing a course of education which is classed as full-time, it means you are not eligible for Carer's Allowance. You may be eligible after your course ends.

Other support available

You can get practical help, advice and support about being a carer at Carers UK (opens in a new tab) and Carers Trust (opens in a new tab).

You could also ask someone at your place of education, such as student services or student counselling, about what help may be available to you as a carer.
