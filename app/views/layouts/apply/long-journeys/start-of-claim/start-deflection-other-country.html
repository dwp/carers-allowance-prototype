Based on this answer, you may not be eligible for Carer's Allowance

You can only get Carer's Allowance if you normally live in England, Wales or Northern Ireland. You might get it if you live in the European Economic Area (EEA) or Switzerland, but you must apply to find out.

Do you still want to apply?

You are still legally entitled to apply, even though you may not qualify for Carer's Allowance.
