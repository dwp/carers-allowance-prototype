You may be allowed Carer’s Allowance

If you expect to earn over £849.37 in the next month, you cannot get Carer's Allowance.

You may be allowed if you have:

  • expenses
  • care costs
  • private pension contributions

These could all reduce your earnings.

You can continue with your application if your earnings vary and some months you get more than £849.37. You will have to tell us your earnings regularly.
